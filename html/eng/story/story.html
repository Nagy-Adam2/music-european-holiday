Music holiday story.

Today, the music festival has become European and even international in size, as it is held on June 21 in more than 100 countries. The Music Festival covers all forms and styles of music. Music museums and music schools across Europe are opening their doors, with rock musicians, DJs, jazz artists, classical music ensembles, and folk singers alternating on outdoor and club stages. Primarily an outdoor event that takes place on streets, squares, public parks, playgrounds. Provided that the rule of gratuity is respected, enclosed spaces can only be used. The European Music Festival also provides an opportunity to open up venues that are not traditional concert venues, e.g. museums, hospitals, public buildings. Hungary joined the movement in 1997, guided by free, united, and voluntary. And the goal is to have everything about music at the same time, across the country and beyond our borders on this day.
