Music holiday about-us.

In 1982, French musicologist Maurice Fleuret suggested that the first day of the summer be greeted with music all over the world.

The Music Festival was first held in France in 1982.

The idea was implemented with the support of French Culture Minister Jack Lang.

The Charter of the European Music Festival was signed in 1997 by 16 cities from 12 European countries in Budapest.
